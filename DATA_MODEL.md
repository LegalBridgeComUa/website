# LegalBridge — Data Model

## Purpose

This document describes the current operational data model used by LegalBridge.

The current operational source of truth is Google Sheets CRM.

Future source of truth will be Supabase.

This document is intended to help developers and AI coding agents understand the structure of the system without needing access to real client data.

---

## Core Tables

Current system tables:

- Orders
- Services
- Service_Content
- Customers
- Pricing
- Tasks
- Files (planned)

---

## 1. Orders

Orders is the main operational table.

Every request from any channel must eventually become an Order.

Sources may include:

- website_form

## Orders Field Meaning

### Created_At
Date and time when the order was created.

Automatically generated by script for manual entries.

Future:
generated by backend.

---

### Customer_Name
Name of the customer or partner.

Can be:
- existing B2B customer
- one-time B2C customer

---

### Display_Short
Human-friendly short service label.

Used by operators when selecting a service.

Example:
UA (стд, ап)

---

### Subject_Full_Name
Full name of the person for whom the document is being ordered.

This may be different from Customer_Name.

---

### Status

Order workflow status.

Allowed values:

- new
- waiting_info
- in_progress
- submitted
- ready
- completed
- cancelled

Meaning:

new = newly created order  
waiting_info = waiting for missing documents or information  
in_progress = being prepared internally  
submitted = submitted to provider / institution / processing channel  
ready = result is ready  
completed = fully completed / delivered / closed  
cancelled = cancelled  

---

### Checked

Manual operator check.

Boolean / checkbox.

Meaning:
- FALSE = not yet manually checked
- TRUE = operator has checked the order data

---

### Ready

Boolean / checkbox.

Indicates whether the document/result is ready.

When TRUE, Ready_At is set automatically.

---

### Ready_At

Date and time when Ready became TRUE.

Automatically generated.

---

### Price_UAH

Final calculated UAH price for the order.

Includes:
- base price from Pricing
- customer discount from Customers

---

### Price_EUR

Final calculated EUR price for the order.

Includes:
- base price from Pricing
- customer discount from Customers

---

### Payment_Status

Payment workflow status.

Allowed values:

- unpaid
- invoiced
- partial
- paid

Meaning:

unpaid = not paid  
invoiced = included in invoice/report  
partial = partially paid  
paid = fully paid  

---

### Order_ID

Unique sequential order identifier.

Example:
ORD-202604-0001

Generated automatically.

---

### Source

Order source.

Examples:
- website_form
- telegram_bot
- viber_bot
- viber_manual
- telegram_manual
- whatsapp_manual
- instagram_manual
- manual

---

### Customer_ID

Auto-linked customer ID from Customers table.

Can be empty for one-time B2C customers.

---

### Customer_Type

Customer type.

Common values:
- b2b
- b2c

For unknown/manual customers, default can be b2c.

---

### Phone_Auto

Phone automatically pulled from Customers.

Used for existing customers.

---

### Phone

Manual phone field.

Used especially for one-time B2C customers.

---

### Service_ID

Operational service variant ID.

Pulled from Services.

Example:
ua_std_ap

---

### Service_Group

Service group.

Examples:
- ua_clearance
- pl_clearance
- hu_clearance
- cz_clearance
- civil_registry
- driver_registry
- education_apostille
- translation

---

### Service_Name

Full operational service name.

Pulled from Services.

---

### Urgency

Service urgency.

Examples:
- normal
- urgent

---

### Apostille

Whether the service includes apostille.

Examples:
- yes
- no

---

### Translation

Whether the service includes translation.

Examples:
- yes
- no
- none

---

### Translation_Lang

Translation language.

Examples:
- SK
- CZ
- -

---

### Extract_Type

Used only for Ukrainian criminal record certificate services.

Relevant only when:

Service_Group = ua_clearance

Allowed values:
- full
- short

Default:
full

---

### Purpose_Code

Used only for Ukrainian criminal record certificate services.

In the current Google Sheets CRM this field stores a human-readable label, not a technical code.

Relevant only when:

Service_Group = ua_clearance

Default:
Подання до установ іноземних держав

Allowed values:
- Подання до установ іноземних держав
- Оформлення візи для виїзду за кордон
- Оформлення на роботу
- Оформлення набуття громадянства
- Усиновлення / опіка / прийомна сім’я
- Публічна закупівля / тендер
- Оформлення дозволу на зброю
- Ліцензія / діяльність з наркотичними речовинами
- Подання до ТЦК та СП
- Пред’явлення за місцем вимоги

Future backend may map these labels to machine codes.

---

### Intake_Details

Structured additional client-provided data.

Used for data that is not a document and not a simple note.

Examples:
- birth surname
- place of birth
- mother’s maiden name
- registration address
- country of submission
- transliteration notes
- additional data for foreign criminal record certificates

---

### Note

Internal operator note.

Used for internal comments, not structured client intake.

## 5. Pricing

Pricing stores the price matrix.

It is component-based.

One service can have multiple price rows.

---

## Pricing Columns

- Price_ID
- Service_ID
- Price_Tier
- Component
- Amount
- Currency
- Active
- Notes

---

## Pricing Logic

One row = one pricing component.

A combined service can have:

- document component in UAH
- translation component in EUR

Example:

Service_ID = ua_std_ap_tr_sk

Rows:
- document / 1200 / UAH
- translation / 25 / EUR

Orders calculate:
- Price_UAH
- Price_EUR

separately.

---

- b2b
- b2c

- vip
- partner_plus
- custom

---

## Component

Common values:
- document
- translation

---

## Currency

Common values:
- UAH
- EUR

## Files Purpose

- SmartDoc results

---

## Core Rule

Orders store metadata and workflow.

Files store attachments and generated documents.

Do not mix them.
